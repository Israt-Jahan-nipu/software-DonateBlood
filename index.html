Rules & Cautions for Blood Donation

  • Donor must be physically healthy — no fever, cold, infection, or any illness.
  • No substance abuse — alcohol, drugs, or intoxicating substances are not allowed.
  • Age requirement — age 18–60 years.
  • Weight requirement — minimum weight 50 kg.
  • Donors with infectious diseases such as HIV, Hepatitis B/C, or other contagious diseases are not eligible.
  • Minimum gap between donations — at least 3 months between two blood donations.
